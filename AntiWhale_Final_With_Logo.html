AntiWhale Token (AWT)

A movement against market manipulation. Power to the people, not the whales.

Our Mission

AntiWhale defends small investors against whales with transparent, decentralized, and fair tokenomics.

Core Features

  • Anti-Whale Mechanism: Limits large transactions to prevent price swings.
  • Penalty Sales: High tax for quick sellers, rewards for holders.
  • Whale Alert Bot: Reports suspicious on-chain activity instantly.
  • Transparency Score: Public rating system for wallets based on behavior.

Tokenomics

  • 50% Community Airdrop
  • 20% Liquidity Pool
  • 10% DAO Treasury
  • 10% Developer Team
  • 10% Security & WhaleWatch

Roadmap

  • Phase 2: Airdrop Campaigns, DAO Setup
  • Phase 3: Tech Development (Bot, Transparency System)
